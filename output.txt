<h2>Part 3 of the <em>Handbook on Intellectual Property Information and Documentation</em></h2>
<h3><b>Who are WIPO Standards for?</b></h3>
<p class="lead">WIPO Standards are presented as recommendations and are directed to states and international organizations, in particular to their national or regional intellectual property offices, to the International Bureau of WIPO, and to any other national or international institution interested in intellectual property documentation.</p>
<p class="lead">All documents listed below are in <abbr title="Portable Document Format">PDF</abbr> format.</p>
